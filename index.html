Rasterizer: DDA & Scanline Algorithms

A rasterizer that uses a .txt file as input and outputs a .png file. Implements DDA and Scanline algorithms, along with other algorithms for creating frustum, culling effects, and more.

A WebGL program that utilizes shaders for creating captivating animation effects. Explore the power of WebGL in generating visually stunning animations.

WebGL Terrain Modeling with Fractal

Delve into WebGL terrain modeling using a fracture-based fractal approach. Witness the creation of intricate landscapes through the power of WebGL.
